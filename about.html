    Comprehensive Therapeutic Services

    I offer personalised mental health support through therapeutic approaches. Below you'll find my specialised therapies, areas of expertise, and flexible scheduling options to meet your needs.


    My Therapeutic Approaches

    Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

    Identifies and modifies negative thought patterns to improve emotional regulation and develop coping strategies. Effective for anxiety, depression, and stress.

    £45-55/session

    EMDR Therapy

    Processes traumatic memories using bilateral stimulation to reduce their emotional impact. Particularly effective for PTSD and trauma recovery.

    £45-55/session

    Psychodynamic Therapy

    Explores unconscious patterns from childhood that influence current behavior and relationships. Helps gain deeper self-understanding.

    £45-55/session

    Solution-Focused Therapy

    Goal-oriented approach focusing on solutions rather than problems. Ideal for short-term work and specific challenges.

    £45-55/session

    Mindfulness-Based Therapy

    Incorporates meditation and present-moment awareness to manage stress, anxiety, and emotional reactivity.

    £45-55/session
