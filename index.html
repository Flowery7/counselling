    About Me

    Compassionate care in the heart of Sowerby Bridge

    I have a Post Graduate Diploma in Counselling and Psychotherapy and a Diploma in Counselling Children and Young People. My training involved 70 hours of personal therapy, 100 hours of placement and close supervision of at least 1 hour to 6 client hours. I am currently working as an EAP Helpline Counsellor, I am also a helpline Counsellor for a small crisis company, which specialises in trauma aftercare. My background is mainly in care and veterinary nursing, always using transferable skills I gained from my psychodynamic counselling skills training. I have worked with young people and adults in care, working various roles with adults and children with disabilities, mental ill health and neurodivergence. I currently volunteer in a school with Place2Be working with Primary School Children.

    Other training and qualifications include: Counselling Skills,BA degree in Drama, Apprenticeship Diploma Level 3 Residential Childcare Level 2 Understanding Children & Young Peoples Mental Health, Level 2 Introduction to Neuroscience

    Registered Member of the BACP

    I believe in the power of therapy to transform lives. It could be anxiety, depression, questions around identity, self esteem, or you could be going through a difficult time, please don't hesitate to reach out.

    My practice is built on trust, empathy, and evidence-based techniques that have helped hundreds of clients find clarity and peace in their lives. Find peace in your journey with me.
